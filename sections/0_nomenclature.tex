\section*{Nomenclature}
\label{sec_nomenclature}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Nomenclature}

{\footnotesize
\begin{tabular}{@{}p{0.26\textwidth}p{0.68\textwidth}@{}}
\multicolumn{2}{@{}l}{\textit{Abbreviations}} \\[0.35em]
AAA & Arrange--act--assert: a unit-test structure that separates setup, execution, and assertions. \\
CI/CD & Continuous integration and continuous delivery: automated build and test on each change. \\
CLI & Command-line interface: flags and subcommands for pipeline tools. \\
FN & False negative: a ground-truth object missed by the model. \\
FP & False positive: a detection not supported by ground truth. \\
GDPR & General Data Protection Regulation: European Union regulation on personal data processing. \\
GPU & Graphics processing unit: hardware used for accelerated training or inference. \\
IDE & Integrated development environment: editor with language services and debugging support. \\
IoU & Intersection over union: overlap measure for matching predictions to labels. \\
JSON & JavaScript Object Notation: structured report format emitted by several CLIs. \\
KPI & Key performance indicator: summary metrics such as detection rate or mask area fraction. \\
mAP & Mean average precision: validation metric for detection and segmentation quality. \\
MPS & Metal Performance Shaders: Apple GPU backend used by PyTorch on macOS. \\
NMS & Non-maximum suppression: post-processing that removes overlapping detections. \\
ONNX & Open Neural Network Exchange: portable model format produced by export. \\
OpenCV & Open Source Computer Vision Library: used for video frame I/O and blur in the pipeline. \\
PR & Pull request: proposed merge of a branch into \texttt{main} on GitHub. \\
PolyForm NC & PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0: noncommercial open-source license for this repository. \\
UV & Astral \texttt{uv}: Python package and virtual-environment manager used per package. \\
YAML & YAML Ain't Markup Language: configuration format for \texttt{dataset.yaml}. \\
YOLO & You Only Look Once: object-detection and segmentation model family (YOLO26-seg here). \\
egui & Immediate-mode GUI library used by the Rust segmentator. \\
\end{tabular}
